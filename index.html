What is nadi astrolgy?

Nadi astrology is an ancient Indian system of astrology that predicts a person's life based on ancient palm leaf manuscripts written by sages. To get a reading, an astrologer matches a person's thumbprint to a specific set of palm leaves that contain predictions about their past, present, and future.

What is Agastya Nadi Jyotish?

It is an ancient astrology method using palm leaves written by sages like Agastya. It reveals your past, present, and future using thumb impressions.
